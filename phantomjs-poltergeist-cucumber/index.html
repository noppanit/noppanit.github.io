PhantomJs + Poltergeist + Cucumber.

Dec 10th, 2012 12:00 am

I love tests and I love experimenting with lots of things. So, I have just come across with PhantomJs a couple weeks ago and I absolutely love it. This should be included in every pipeline. No matter what you want to do, Jasmine? Acceptance Testing? I think right now PhantomJs is the most suitable one (May be there’ll be another one coming). But this post is not really about PhantomJs. I just want to show what it can do by using Poltergeist to run cucumber test headlessly. Of course you can do it with selenium-driver but running the test headlessly seem to be faster. So, I’ll just gonna jump right in.

First thing first, install PhantomJs from here
