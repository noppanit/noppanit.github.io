Jan 16th, 2011 12:00 am

Ever wonder why dummy text or banner text on web templates or Microsoft Word Templates has to begin with “Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet…” and go on and on. Do you even know what this means? Well I don’t. But I found an answer that might explain all of this.

This is what they say “The point of using Lorem Ipsum is that it has a more-or-less normal distribution of letters, as opposed to using ‘Content here, content here’, making it look like readable English. Many desktop publishing packages and web page editors now use Lorem Ipsum as their default model text, and a search for ‘lorem ipsum’ will uncover many web sites still in their infancy. Various versions have evolved over the years, sometimes by accident, sometimes on purpose (injected humour and the like)”
